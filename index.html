Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions about Our BIM Services

  • What is BIM?

    Building Information Modeling (BIM) is a digital representation of the physical and functional characteristics of a building. It provides a shared knowledge resource for information about a facility, forming a reliable basis for decisions during its lifecycle from inception onward.

  • Why should I use BIM for my project?

    BIM enhances collaboration, improves accuracy, and increases efficiency throughout the project lifecycle. It allows for better visualization, clash detection, cost estimation, and project management, ultimately leading to time and cost savings.

  • What software do your BIM Modelers use?

    Our BIM Modelers use leading BIM software such as Autodesk Revit, Navisworks, and BIM 360 to create and manage detailed and accurate models.

  • What are the benefits of using custom Revit Add-ins?

    Custom Revit Add-ins automate repetitive tasks, enhance design capabilities, and streamline workflows, significantly reducing the time and effort required to complete various project tasks. This leads to increased efficiency, accuracy, and cost savings.

  • Do you offer support and training for the BIM models and add-ins you provide?

    Yes, we offer comprehensive support and training to ensure that your team can effectively use the BIM models and add-ins we provide. Our ongoing support includes addressing any issues and providing updates as needed.
